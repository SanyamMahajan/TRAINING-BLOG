Task Execution & Challenges

Today was purely focused on practicing array-related problems with different patterns such as two pointers, divide & conquer, and dynamic programming. I attempted and solved the following problems:

  • 🔁 Merge Overlapping Intervals — Understanding sorting and interval merging logic was important here.
  • 🧩 Merge Two Sorted Arrays without Extra Space — Solved with a two-pointer strategy in-place.
  • ⚠️ Reverse Pairs — Required applying a modified merge sort logic. This was tricky and took time to debug.
  • 💥 Maximum Product Subarray — A classic DP problem where tracking both max and min was essential.

Challenges faced:

  • 🔍 Reverse Pairs problem was difficult due to its divide and conquer approach and edge case handling.
  • 🧮 For Maximum Product Subarray, I had to revisit the concept of maintaining both max and min at every index since negative numbers can flip the result.
  • 🔧 Merge Intervals was easier conceptually but needed precision in implementation.
