Task Execution & Challenges

I kicked off by thoroughly analyzing the challenge requirements, layout, and component breakdown. It’s a ticket generator UI, but with dynamic and conditional sections — a great way to brush up on DOM manipulation and layout structuring.

Here are a few challenges I encountered today:

Despite these challenges, it was a great learning experience refining the UI structure and applying responsive design techniques.
