Task Execution & Challenges

Most of the day was spent refining my portfolio website — integrating smooth scroll, finalizing mobile responsiveness, aligning cards, and adjusting typography. I also spent time polishing transitions and accessibility details for better user experience.

💡 One of the interesting things I tried was experimenting with new color schemes and adding interactive feedback on hover for various sections. Although small, these changes added a nice touch to the overall feel.

Challenges faced:

  • 🔄 Trying to balance minimalism with aesthetic UI was tricky.
  • 📋 Also had to make sure content was up-to-date and organized without cluttering.
