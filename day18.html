Today's focus was on two key areas:

Task Execution & Challenges

Started the day by fine-tuning my portfolio layout. Focused on improving responsiveness across screen sizes, making sure the design adapts well on tablets and mobile phones. I reviewed spacing, font sizes, button alignment, and overall user flow.
